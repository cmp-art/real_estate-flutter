## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

```
Patamjengo is Tanzania's trusted real estate platform — making it easy to find, list, and manage properties from anywhere.

🏠 FIND YOUR PERFECT PROPERTY
Browse thousands of residential and commercial listings across Tanzania. Filter by location, price, bedrooms, bathrooms, and property type to find exactly what you're looking for. View high-quality photos, virtual tours, and detailed descriptions.

📍 LOCATION-POWERED SEARCH
Use our interactive map to explore properties in any neighbourhood. See listings near your current location or search any area across the country.

💰 BUY, SELL, OR RENT
Whether you're a buyer, seller, landlord, or tenant — Patamjengo has you covered. List your property in minutes and reach thousands of potential buyers or renters.

🔔 INSTANT ALERTS
Get notified the moment a property matching your criteria becomes available. Never miss out on the perfect home or investment opportunity.

💬 DIRECT MESSAGING
Connect directly with property owners, agents, and buyers through our built-in chat system. Ask questions, schedule viewings, and negotiate — all in one place.

💳 SECURE PAYMENTS
Pay securely using Selcom mobile money or card payments. Our secure payment system protects every transaction.

🔐 VERIFIED LISTINGS
We verify property listings to reduce fraud and give you confidence when browsing. Look for the verified badge on listings.

📱 KEY FEATURES
• Search properties by location, price, size, and type
• Interactive map view with property pins
• High-resolution photos and video tours
• Save favourites and compare properties
• Direct messaging with owners and agents
• Push notifications for new matches
• Google Sign-In for quick, secure access
• Works in both English and Swahili

Whether you're buying your first home, investing in real estate, or searching for the perfect rental — Patamjengo makes property discovery simple, transparent, and stress-free.

Download now and start your property journey today.
```
